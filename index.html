Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

Can you actually fix my credit?

We provide financial education and guide you through the process — you're empowered to take the right steps. While we can't guarantee specific score changes, clients who follow our strategic roadmap consistently see meaningful improvement over 90–180 days.

How much does it cost to get started?

Your initial consultation is completely free. We believe you should understand your options before you invest anything. From there, services are priced based on your specific situation and goals.

Do you serve clients outside of New Jersey?

Yes — we serve clients across all 50 states. Everything is handled remotely, so your location is never a barrier to getting started.

How long does credit repair typically take?

Most clients begin seeing measurable changes within 60–90 days. Significant improvement typically occurs over 6–12 months depending on the complexity of your credit history and your consistency in implementing the strategies.

What debt relief options do you help with?

We educate clients on a full range of options including debt management plans, negotiation strategies, consolidation, and hardship programs. We help you understand which path fits your financial picture — without pressure.

How does business funding education work?

We teach you how to properly structure your business, establish business credit separate from personal credit, and position your company to qualify for real funding. Most clients are ready for their first business credit line within 90 days of following the system.
