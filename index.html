Common Questions

What people
want to know.

Do you fix my credit for me?

No — and that’s actually a good thing. We educate you on how credit works, what’s on your report, and what actions you can take. No one can legally remove accurate negative information from your report, and any company claiming otherwise should be a red flag. We help you build real, lasting credit through strategy and education.

Is there a cost for the initial consultation?

The initial consultation is always free. We want to understand your situation before recommending any service. No commitment required to have a conversation.

Do you work with clients outside of New Jersey?

Yes — all 50 states. We operate remotely and our consultations take place by phone or video. Your location doesn’t limit what we can do for you.

How long does it take to see credit improvement?

It depends on your starting point. Some clients see changes within 30–60 days once they begin taking the right steps. Others take 6–12 months to reach their goals. We give you an honest picture of your specific situation from day one.

What’s the difference between debt relief and debt consolidation?

Debt consolidation combines multiple debts into one payment, often at a lower interest rate. Debt relief typically involves negotiating to reduce or settle what you owe. We walk you through both approaches and help you determine which fits your situation — without any sales pressure.

Can you help me get business funding even if my personal credit isn’t great?

Yes. We focus on building your business credit profile independently of your personal score — and we teach you what lenders actually look for when evaluating a business. A strong personal score helps, but it’s not the only path to funding.
